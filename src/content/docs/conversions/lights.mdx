<MappingNotes>

- `new THREE.AmbientLight()` becomes `<ambientLight />`.
- `new THREE.DirectionalLight()` becomes `<directionalLight />`.
- Position and intensity stay conceptually identical, but they become props.
- The object being lit does not change. Only the scene authoring style changes.

</MappingNotes>

## Practical Rule

Whenever a Three.js example adds a light to the scene, your first R3F instinct should be to look for the matching lowercase JSX primitive before reaching for abstractions.
